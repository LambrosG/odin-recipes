Moussaka

Moussaka is an eggplant- or potato-based dish, often including ground meat, which is common in the Balkans and the Middle East, with many local and regional variations. Extremely famous dish in Greece.

Prep & Cook Details

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Wait Time: 30 mins

Cool Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 1 hrs 40 mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 500 Grams Minced Meat Cooked
  • 100 Grams Grated Parmesan
  • 30 Grams Parsley Chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Grams of Ground Nutmeg
  • 1 Kilo of Eggplants
  • 1 Kilo of Potatoes
  • 300 Grams of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Kilo of Bechamel
  • 10 Grams of Salt and Pepper

Directions

Step 1

Cut the eggplants into thin slices and put them in cold salted water for about half an hour.

Step 2

Flour the eggplants and fry them.

Step 3

Cut the potatoes into thin slices, fry them, and drain in a colander so that they don't absorb too much oil.

Step 4

Add half the parmesan cheese, the eggs, the chopped parsley, and the grated nutmeg to the cooked minced meat.

Step 5

Put a layer of potato, a layer of minced eggplant and another layer of eggplant on a baking sheet.

Step 6

Season each layer lightly with salt and pepper and add grated parmesan.

Step 7

At the end, add the bechamel, making sure it is not too tight.

Step 8

Add some more grated parmesan and bake in the oven at 160 degrees fot about 30 to 40 minutes.
